## 1. Be Respectful

Treat all players with respect. Harassment, discrimination, and hate speech
are not tolerated.

## 2. No Griefing

Do not destroy or modify other players' builds without their permission.
This includes stealing from chests and killing pets.

## 3. No Cheating

Cheating, hacking, or exploiting bugs is strictly prohibited. This includes:

- X-ray texture packs
- Auto-clickers and macros
- Duplication glitches
- Any mod that gives an unfair advantage

## 4. No Spamming

Do not spam chat, voice channels, or signs. Keep communication
reasonable and on-topic.

## 5. Respect Staff Decisions

Staff members have the final say. If you disagree with a decision,
appeal respectfully through the designated channels.
