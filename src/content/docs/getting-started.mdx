## Welcome to TitanSMP

TitanSMP is a synchronized Minecraft network where your progress follows you
across SMPs, hubs, and future duel arenas.

### What Makes TitanSMP Special

Unlike traditional Minecraft servers, TitanSMP synchronizes your inventory,
ender chest, and more across multiple connected worlds. You can explore
different SMPs without losing your gear.

### Quick Start

1. Make sure you have a **Minecraft Java Edition** account (1.21+).
2. Check the [How to Join](/docs/how-to-join) guide for connection details.
3. Read the [Rules](/docs/rules) before you start playing.
