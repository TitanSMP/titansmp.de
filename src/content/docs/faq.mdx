## General Questions

### What is TitanSMP?

TitanSMP is a synchronized Minecraft network. Your inventory, ender chest,
and stats are shared across all connected servers.

### Is the server free?

Yes, TitanSMP is completely free to play.

### What Minecraft version do I need?

You need Minecraft Java Edition **1.21 or later**.

## Gameplay

### Does my inventory sync across servers?

Yes! Your inventory and ender chest contents are synchronized across all
SMPs and hubs on the network.

### Can I keep my items when moving between SMPs?

Absolutely. That is the core feature of TitanSMP — your progress follows
you wherever you go.

### Are there any plans for minigames or PvP arenas?

Yes, duel arenas and other minigames are planned for future updates.
